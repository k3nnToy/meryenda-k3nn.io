About Meryenda sa Kanto PH

Meryenda sa Kanto PH is your go-to portal for exploring and celebrating the delicious world of traditional Filipino snacks. Our mission is to rekindle fond memories, share cultural heritage, and introduce new generations to the joy of Filipino meryenda (snack time) favorites!

From the colorful kakanin, to the sizzling street foods, to the warm bakery classics, we bring you closer to the tastes that make the Philippines vibrant and unforgettable.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Filipino snack culture, one delicious bite at a time. We aim to make these iconic treats accessible and appreciated by both locals and food enthusiasts around the world.

Our Vision

To become the leading online hub for Filipino food traditions, inspiring pride in Filipino culinary artistry and strengthening cultural connections through food.
